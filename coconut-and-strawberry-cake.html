Coconut and Strawberry Cake

Back to Recipes

Ingredients

  • 300g margarine
  • 300g caster sugar
  • 300g self-raising flour
  • 5 large eggs
  • 8 tbsp milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 100g dessicated coconut (extra for cake topping)
  • Jam

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C/160°C Fan/350ºF
  2. Cream the margarine and sugar together until smooth
  3. Add the eggs and mix well
  4. Once eggs have been added, add in the flour and mix until smooth
  5. Then add the vanilla extract and mix well
  6. Add the milk and mix
  7. Add dessicated coconut and ensure mixture is smooth
  8. Pour the micture into a square tray and bake 30-45 minutes, until cake is golden brown
  9. Leave cake to cool in tin for 15 minutes before turning it out into a wire rack
  10. Once the cake has fully cooled down, spread the jam evenly across the top of the cake
  11. Sprinkle the rest of the dessicated coconut across the cake
  12. Enjoy!
