Chocolate Chip Cookies

Back to Recipes

Ingredients

  • 175g self-raising flour
  • 100g plain flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 150g unsalted butter
  • 200g brown sugar
  • 100g caster sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 eggs
  • 100g semi-sweet chocolate

Instructions

  1. Melt butter over the stove. While whisking continue to cook butter until it foams the subsides. When melted, pour itno a bowl to avoid cooking any further and burning, allow to cool for 15 minutes.
  2. Combine brown butter, brown sugar and caster sugar in a bowl and stir together until it resembles wet sand.
  3. Add in egg and vanilla extract, and stir until smooth
  4. Sift flour and baking soda into wet ingredients and gently fold in. Take care not to over mix.
  5. Fold in your chocolate chunks
  6. Cover and chill dough in the fridge for at least 3 hours.
  7. Preheat oven to 180°C/160°C Fan/350ºF
  8. Scoop out mounds of dough onto a baking sheet, then bake for 12-15 minutes or until the edges of your cookies are browned.
  9. Allow to cool and enjoy!
